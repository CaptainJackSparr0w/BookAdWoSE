  • 1. Start from Greetings, Gratitude, and Regrets, etc.
  • 2. Start from sentences of only one or two words and go on step by step - e.g. sentences - कुरसी मंगाइ है, बत्ती है।
  • 3. Add one or two words and learn the next pattern of sentences easily.
  • 4. It includes all types of sentences - Tenses - without main verb, with main verb in active & in passive voice, imperatives, multiple uses of infinitives, causatives, modals, & so on.

Separate conversations on each type of sentence for your practice with examples and hints for your guidance, then two or more types of sentences in further conversations.
All the conversations include different matters, like greetings, regrets, shopping of clothes, groceries, shoes, jewelry, talks on agriculture, gardening, diseases, festivals, fireworks, fairs, music, makeup, office work, judicial process, accidents, driving, reporting, activities of animals, insects, bees, and so on. Round the Table Conversation is for the practice of speaking with your friends, with all types of sentences.
